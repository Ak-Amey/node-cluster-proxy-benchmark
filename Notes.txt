Microservices architecture: An approach where an application is built as a collection of small, independent services that communicate over APIs. Each service focuses on a specific business function and can be developed, deployed, and scaled independently.

Monolithic architecture: An approach where an application is built as a single, unified unit. All components (UI, business logic, data access) are tightly coupled and run as one process, making development and deployment simpler but less flexible for scaling and updates.
